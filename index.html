About Us

Computer Society of Uva Wellassa University is run by the students with the involvement and the guidance of the staff. It was established to provide mainly computer science related services to the university and its students. This society is directly connected with the department of Computer Science and informatics in Factulty of Applied Sciences. It is mainly focused on the development of the technical skills of the students regarding the IT field.

Focus On What Matters Most

Currently this society is actively working to promote the professional and soft skills of the undergraduate students who mainly study in computer science and technology and industrial information technology. But even the others who study in the faculty of applies sciennces can join and work actively in this community if they are interested in the particular area.

Further more main aim of this society is to generate a Geek who is talented in many computer related areas such as,

  • Programming
  • Computer networking
  • Computer hardware and etc.

And Undergraduated will get many helps to build up their technical skills through many workshops, programs and awareness sessions conducted by this society
